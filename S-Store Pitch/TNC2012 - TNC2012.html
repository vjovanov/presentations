Speaker details

AMRES / University of Belgrade (Serbia)

Branko Marović

Branko Marović received his BS, MS and PhD degrees in computer engineering and computer science from University of Belgrade. He is with the Belgrade University Computer Center since 1994, currently leading its Center for Software Development. He also worked at UCLA Radiology Science Department.

Branko's primary work is in areas of medical informatics, computing infrastructures, and management of software projects. He is currently leading the GEANT task on software related best practices, which includes promotion of agile in GN3 software development and operation/support for software-based services. He is also teaching MS and PhD courses on medical informatics and virtual reality at the University of Belgrade and University of Banja Luka.

Branko Marović is a speaker in the following presentations:

  • Bringing governance into distributed R&D software development - GÉANT case study which is part of session Security and Governance
